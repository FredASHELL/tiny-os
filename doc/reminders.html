Before You Ask...

There are a few things you should know about TinyOS.

  • Root and User accounts do not exist.
  • Sudo does not exist.
  • There is no GUI.
  • There is not really a UI in that sense.
  • Linux commands do not work.
  • macOS commands do not work.
  • Windows commands do not work.
  • Except for clear.
  • Elevated permissions are unnecessary.
  • It is very bare-bones.
  • It is built with NASM.
  • It is a 16-bit operating system.
  • It is not based on Linux.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is sudo?
A: There isn't one.

Q: How do I become root?
A: You can't.

Q: Where is the GUI?
A: There isn't one.

Q: Why doesn't ls work?
A: Wrong operating system.

Q: Is it Linux?
A: No.

Q: Will it follow UNIX philosophy?
A: No.

Final Reminder

TinyOS is its own thing. Please adjust your expectations accordingly.
